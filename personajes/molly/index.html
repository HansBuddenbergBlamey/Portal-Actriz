Helix Tennis

Meet Helix! The new racket game that combines the best elements of badminton and beach ball.

In the first stages of development by student graduates from the Erasmus university in Rotterdam, Woodwork has played a part in providing a product video explaining the ins and outs of the product to attract investors.

After a successful product launch we are ready to go all out with a new TV commercial. Sit back and enjoy the revival of these retro graphics that will display the revolutionary awesomeness of Helix.
